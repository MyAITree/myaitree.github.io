Welcome to the Reading Flow Highlighter. This tool helps you read text more efficiently by providing a smooth, continuous highlight that flows through the text based on your mouse movement speed.

As you move your mouse horizontally, the highlight will progress through the text. Move faster to progress more quickly, or slower for a more leisurely pace. The controls above allow you to adjust the sensitivity and speed to match your reading preference.

This technique can help improve reading speed and comprehension by guiding your eyes through the text in a consistent flow. It's particularly useful for dense or complex material where maintaining focus is important.

Try adjusting the settings to find what works best for you. You can always reset to the default values if needed. The goal is to create a comfortable, natural reading rhythm that matches your personal pace.

How to Use

  • Move mouse horizontally to control the highlight flow
  • Adjust sensitivity to change how mouse movement affects highlighting
  • Adjust highlight speed to control the base progression rate
  • Click Start to begin the highlighting flow
  • Double-click anywhere to show/hide the cursor
