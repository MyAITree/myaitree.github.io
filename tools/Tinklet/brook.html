Welcome to ReadFlow Pro Focus Mode, the revolutionary reading assistant that eliminates distractions and keeps your eyes focused on what's ahead. With our advanced text fading and hiding features, previously read content gradually disappears from view, preventing your eyes from wandering back.

The science behind this approach is clear: by reducing visual clutter and eliminating regression (the tendency to re-read text), reading speed and comprehension can improve significantly. Our focus modes help train your eyes to move forward consistently.

Choose between three focus modes: Normal displays all text normally, Fade gradually grays out text you've already read, and Hide completely conceals previous text to create a truly forward-moving reading experience.

Research in reading psychology shows that minimizing visual distractions can improve reading speed by up to 40% while maintaining or even improving comprehension. The focused approach helps break the habit of subvocalization and regression.

This is particularly valuable for speed reading practice, studying dense material, or any situation where maintaining forward momentum is crucial. The technology works seamlessly with our infinite scrolling to create an uninterrupted reading flow.

As you progress through the text, notice how your focus naturally remains ahead of the highlight. The disappearing text creates a psychological incentive to keep moving forward, training your brain to process information more efficiently.

Experiment with the different focus modes to discover which works best for your reading style and the material you're working with. Many users find they can gradually increase their reading speed as they adapt to the focused environment.
