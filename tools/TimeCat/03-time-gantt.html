How to use

  • Use the form above to create new tasks with start and end dates
  • Change the view mode using the options above the chart
  • Click on a task in the Gantt chart to view its details
  • Drag the edges of tasks in the chart to change their duration
  • Drag the middle of tasks to change their start date
  • Use the navigation buttons to move through the timeline
